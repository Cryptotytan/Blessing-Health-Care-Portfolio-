About Me

Bridging Clinical Excellence with Technology

I am a dedicated Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife with a deep passion for transforming healthcare through innovation, data analytics, and cutting-edge technology. Currently serving at Lagoon Hospitals, one of Nigeria's premier healthcare institutions, I deliver compassionate, evidence-based care while championing digital transformation in clinical practice.

My journey in healthcare extends beyond bedside care. As an emerging researcher, I explore how data-driven insights, artificial intelligence, and early warning systems can revolutionize patient safety and outcomes. I'm particularly passionate about maternal health, emergency response, and leveraging technology to bridge gaps in healthcare delivery across Nigeria.

Beyond clinical duties, I actively contribute to public health awareness, particularly in areas like Hepatitis prevention and healthcare education. I believe in the power of knowledge sharing and community engagement to create lasting health impact.
